About Lion

The lion, known as the king of the jungle, is a powerful and majestic animal that lives in prides. They are found mainly in Africa and are symbols of courage and strength.

About Tiger

Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world, known for their striking orange coats with black stripes. They are solitary hunters and found across Asia.

About Elephant

Elephants are the largest land mammals, known for their intelligence, memory, and strong family bonds. They live in herds and are native to Africa and Asia.
